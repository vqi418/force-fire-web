Force and Fire is a duly chartered body of Ordo Templi Orientis in the Valley of London, Ontario.

The O.T.O. is a hierarchical, fraternal organization. Our mission is to effect and promote the doctrines and practices of the philosophical and religious system known as Thelema, with particular emphasis on cultivating the ideals of individual liberty, self-discipline, self-knowledge, and universal brotherhood. To this end, we conduct sacramental and initiatory rites, offer guidance and instruction to our members and organize social and educational events.

Our public events are accessible through our workgroup London Ontario Thelema.

More information on the Order and Thelema can be found on the "About OTO" section of this website, and the National and International web pages.
