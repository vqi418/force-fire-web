  • Liber CI - An Open Letter to those who may wish to join the Order
    These regulations first appeared in The Equinox III(1) (Detroit: Universal, 1919) and constitute our best and most comprehensive guidelines for Thelemic social intercourse. Certain provisions will need to be modified to take advantage of the U.S.A.'s comparatively enlightened tax-exemption statutes as applied to religious organizations—a few are of dubious legality at this writing. Most of the principles outlined herein have long been observed in the U.S. O.T.O. —Hymenaeus Beta O.H.O.
